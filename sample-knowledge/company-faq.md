# Acme Gadgets — Customer FAQ & Policies

## About Acme Gadgets
Acme Gadgets is an online electronics store founded in 2019, based in Bengaluru, India.
We sell smartphones, wireless earbuds, smartwatches, power banks, and laptop accessories.
Our mission is to make quality gadgets affordable for everyone across India.

## Orders and Payment
You can place an order through our website or directly over WhatsApp.
We accept UPI, credit cards, debit cards, net banking, and Cash on Delivery (COD).
Cash on Delivery is available for orders under 5000 rupees in select pin codes.
Once an order is placed, you will receive an order confirmation with an order number over WhatsApp.
Orders can be cancelled free of charge any time before they are shipped.

## Shipping and Delivery
Standard shipping is free on all orders above 999 rupees and takes 3 to 5 business days.
For orders below 999 rupees, a flat shipping fee of 60 rupees applies.
Express shipping is available for an extra 150 rupees and delivers within 1 to 2 business days.
We ship across all major cities and most rural pin codes through our courier partners.
After your order ships, you will receive a live tracking link over WhatsApp.

## Returns and Refunds
Customers can request a full refund within 14 days of delivery, no questions asked.
To start a return, reply to your order confirmation on WhatsApp with your order number.
Refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days after we receive the item.
Items must be returned in their original packaging with all accessories included.
Opened or used items may be eligible for a partial refund at the discretion of the support team.
We do not charge any restocking fee for unopened items returned within the refund window.

## Warranty
All smartphones and smartwatches come with a 12 month manufacturer warranty.
Wireless earbuds and power banks come with a 6 month warranty.
The warranty covers manufacturing defects but does not cover physical or water damage.
To claim warranty, contact support with your order number and a short description of the issue.

## Customer Support
Our support team is available Monday to Friday, from 9 AM to 6 PM IST.
On Saturdays we offer limited support from 10 AM to 2 PM IST for urgent issues only.
We are closed on Sundays and all national public holidays.
You can reach us over WhatsApp, email at support@acmegadgets.example, or phone.
Outside business hours, our chatbot can answer common questions automatically.
